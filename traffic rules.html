Taffic Rules

Following traffic signals and road signs helps maintain order and prevents accidents on busy roads. Pedestrians should always use designated crossings and stay alert to oncoming vehicles for their own safety.

Wearing seatbelts in cars or helmets on two-wheelers is essential because they greatly reduce injury during collisions. Drivers must respect speed limits and avoid distractions to ensure a safe and smooth journey for everyone.
